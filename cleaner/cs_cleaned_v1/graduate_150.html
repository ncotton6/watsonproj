Graduate Bridge Exams

The Computer Science department will offer graduate bridge exams for spring enrollment on Monday, August 15th, 2016, starting at 9:00am.  Please check in for all bridge exams in Golisano Hall, room 3005 (the main Computer Science Student Services Office).  Please refer to the following bridge exam schedule for more information:

Bridge Exam Schedule and Information

Graduate Orientation

During the CS Orientation program, we will give an overview of the academic program, structure of the CS Department, and RIT. Your attendance is required as we will conduct course registration for all new graduate students immediately following our Orientation program. You will be registered for spring semester classes by the Graduate Director and Academic Advisors based on your admissions contingencies and success on any bridge exams you opt to take on Monday, August 15th. It is imperative you attend these events so we may verify your enrollment and complete your course registration.

Continue to check email from the Graduate Academic Advisors for helpful answers to your questions regarding how best to prepare for your transition into CS@RIT.
