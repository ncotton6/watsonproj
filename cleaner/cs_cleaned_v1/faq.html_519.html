Frequently Asked Questions
  • Can I apply to the program if I hold a "green card"?
    Yes, the program is open to U.S. citizens and permanent residents. On the application form you will be required to confirm your status.
  • Can I apply to the program if I am a high school student?
    High school graduates who have been accepted at an undergraduate institution but who have not yet started their undergraduate study are eligible to participate.
  • Can I apply to the program if I am a graduate student?
    No, REU Site: Computational Sensing is a research opportunity for undergraduates, and we accept applications from current first-years through seniors.
    You should be enrolled in an undergraduate program in the coming fall semester.
  • Can Rochester Institute of Technology students apply?
    Yes, the REU Site: Computational Sensing program is open to students from RIT, too.
  • Is this a paid position?
    Yes, the participants will receive a $5,000 stipend as well as coverage of the costs for travel to Rochester, New York, and housing is provided.
